# Flagship — Feature List (for listings & marketing)

Premium Online Store 2.0 fashion theme. Single-product or full catalog.
No apps required for any feature below. No jQuery, no external libraries.

## Design & presets
- 3 ready presets: **Lumen** (warm minimal light), **Mode** (black & white
  editorial), **Vanta** (dark + electric accent)
- Token-driven design: colors, 2 font roles, corner radius, spacing, page
  width — change once, restyles everything
- 2 product card styles: standard / caption-bar overlay
- 2 header styles (light/dark) · 2 footer layouts (columns/centered logo)
- Fully responsive, mobile-first; WCAG-minded (focus states, aria,
  contrast-safe presets, reduced-motion support)

## Animations (all GPU-only, reduced-motion safe)
- Live WebGL **3D silk hero** (no libraries, pauses off-screen)
- **3D product spotlight**: color swatches swap the floating image in 3D
- Scroll reveals sitewide: 3D card flip-ins, zooms, left/right slides,
  staggered cascades, auto-animated headings
- Parallax banner imagery · 3D tilt on cards & tiles
- Ambient motion: floating badges & thumbnails, pulsing CTAs, glow orbs,
  cart-count bounce, marquee ticker, drifting watermark hero

## Conversion & merchandising
- Quick view modal · quick add · Buy It Now (dynamic checkout)
- Slide-out AJAX cart + cart page, order notes, free-shipping trust row
- Sticky add-to-cart bar · low-stock urgency counter · pre-order labels
- Sale % badges, sold-out states, vendor lines, color swatch dots on cards
- Variant images switch the gallery; size boxes + round color swatches
- Product gallery: image grid or thumbnail layout, zoom lightbox, video
  support, sticky buy panel, collapsible tabs (size chart/care/shipping),
  back-in-stock email form, in-store pickup display, trust badges,
  floating ghost-image effect (toggle)
- Recommended products (related/complementary + smart fallback)
- Recently viewed · **Wishlist** (hearts on cards/product, wishlist page,
  header count — no app needed)
- Countdown deal banners · promo popup + age verifier · scrolling marquee
- Promo tiles, lookbook image hotspots, before/after slider, video banner,
  3D slideshow with cinematic zoom, editorial category grid, season
  carousel, press logo bar, Instagram-style gallery, testimonials, FAQ
  accordion with categories

## Discovery & navigation
- **Mega menu**: per-item control — any menu item becomes a full-width
  panel with columns + promo image block; others stay compact dropdowns
- Predictive search with live suggestions + full results page
- Collection & search pages: **left filter sidebar** (price, availability,
  color swatches, any option), sorting, active-filter pills, product counts
- Pagination choice: numbered / load-more / infinite scroll
- Breadcrumbs · back-to-top · collection navigation pills · utility bar

## Global selling & SEO
- Shopify Markets ready: country/currency + language selectors
- 5 languages included: EN, FR, DE, ES, IT
- JSON-LD structured data (Product, Organization, Website, Breadcrumbs)
- Open Graph + Twitter cards · semantic headings · lazy images + srcset

## Complete page set
Home · product · collection · collections list · search · cart · blog
(tag filters, stacked editorial cards) · article · pages (About, FAQ,
Contact with info cards, Wishlist templates) · 404 · gift card · full
customer accounts (login, register, orders with tracking links, order
detail, addresses, reset/activate)

## Merchant experience
- 40+ sections, every feature toggleable in the theme editor — zero code
- Looks complete on install: designed placeholder system until real
  content is added
- Setup guide + full documentation included
